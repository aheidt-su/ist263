Welcome to the Syracuse University Wrestling Club!

We are a diverse and dedicated group of student-athletes committed to the art and sport of wrestling. Our club provides opportunities for wrestlers of all skill levels to develop their abilities, gain competitive experience, and build lifelong friendships.

At Syracuse University Wrestling Club, we focus on training and competing throughout the year. We emphasize physical fitness, technique, and sportsmanship. Whether you are new to wrestling or have years of experience, we welcome you to join us.

We train three times a week and participate in collegiate competitions, providing valuable exposure and experience in the sport. Our dedicated coaching staff and experienced team members are here to support and guide you along your wrestling journey.

Interested in joining? Reach out to us via email or message us on Instagram at @syracuseuwrestling!
